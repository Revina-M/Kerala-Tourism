Kozhikode

A great seaport where Arab, Chinese and East African traders once converged,Kozhikode was the important region of the Malabar coast in early days. Vasco da Gama landed on its shores in 1498. Once the capital of the powerful Zamorins and a prominent trade and commerce centre ,the whiff of history continues to permeate the lanes,bazaars and business hubs and also adding to the legacy of this region is a unique culture and warm friendly people.
